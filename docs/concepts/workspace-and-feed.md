# Workspace And Feed

Workspace and feed are Threader's collaboration layer.

Use them when analysis material should become team discussion.

## Workspace

A workspace represents the group of people who can collaborate around shared analysis workflows.

Workspace data includes:

- members
- invitations
- account/profile context
- permissions when available

## Feed

Feed is where shared analysis material becomes a conversation.

Feed items can reference analysis outputs such as clips, playlists, notes, or future dashboard material.

## Practical Rule

Do local analysis first. Publish to feed when there is a clip, playlist, or review item worth discussing.
