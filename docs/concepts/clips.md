# Clips

A clip is a saved video range for analysis and review.

Clips are the bridge between watching video and producing coaching material. They can be created from time ranges or events, then annotated, drawn on, grouped, and shared.

## A Clip Usually Has

- start and end time
- title
- linked events when available
- tactical annotations when needed
- drawings or notes when useful

## Why Clips Matter

Clips make moments durable. Once a moment becomes a clip, you can:

- revisit it quickly
- group it into playlists
- attach tactical meaning
- share it through feed
- use it in review meetings

## Practical Rule

Create clips early. Add more structure only when the clip is worth reviewing or sharing.
