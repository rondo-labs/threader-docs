# Clips and Playlists

Clips are short video segments that preserve a match moment for review, sharing, or presentation. A clip can be created around an event, tagged with players, linked to tactical drawings, and grouped into playlists.

## Clips

Use clips when you want to isolate a moment without losing its connection to the original match. Clips can carry context such as:

- Start and end time
- Linked events
- Player tags
- Tactical annotations
- Export settings

## Playlists

Playlists group multiple clips into a review sequence. A playlist might represent a player review, a training topic, a set-piece package, or a post-match meeting.

## Exports

Threader can export individual clips and playlists through the clip export workflow. Keep clip names descriptive so exported files remain understandable outside the project.
