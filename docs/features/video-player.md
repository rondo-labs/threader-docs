# Video Player

The video player is the center of Threader. It is designed for long review sessions where the analyst needs fast playback control and minimal visual noise.

## Capabilities

- Local video playback
- Variable playback speed
- Frame-level review
- Fullscreen and cinema-style focus modes
- Playback HUD for quick context
- Docked panels that keep the video visible

## Cinema Mode

Cinema Mode hides app chrome so the video can take over the workspace. Use it when reviewing footage live or presenting a moment without side panels.
